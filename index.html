Favorite Websites

Firstly, if you're interested in catching up on worldly news but don't have an episode of the Daily Show recorded or don't have the energy to suffer through a long, monotonous, bland news outlet, check out Slate. If you do, however, have the energy and are looking for a good story without the edginess, head over to NY Times online (nothing compares to the feel of a Newspaper between your hands, but its inefficiency kills). I also really enjoy Memebase, for those interested in the cutting edge of pop culture references and hilarious colloquialisms. Their app, which was on the store for several years, has recently been taken down, but their website works just as well, even on mobile platforms.
